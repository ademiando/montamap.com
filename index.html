About Montamap

Xcapeak is a digital platform redefining outdoor exploration.

We connect hikers, explorers, local guides, and sustainable communities within one intelligent, safe, and responsible ecosystem.

From legendary peaks to hidden trails, we enhance your journey through technology, trust, and purpose.

Vision

To be the leading global adventure platform connecting hikers, local guides, and sustainable communities through an intelligent, safe, and responsible trekking ecosystem.

Mission

  1. Provide an integrated booking and ticket system for trekking destinations worldwide.
  2. Empower porters and local guides through a fair and transparent digital platform.
  3. Educate hikers on safety, trekking ethics, and environmental conservation.
  4. Offer smart navigation tools: offline maps, real-time trail conditions, and SOS emergency features.
  5. Build a global community supporting collaboration, preservation, and responsible exploration.

Our Core Values

  • Adventure with Integrity: We explore with respect for nature, culture, and humanity.
  • Technology for Empowerment: Innovation becomes a tool to enhance the lives of local communities and adventurers.
  • Safety as a Standard: Smart preparation and real-time tools to protect every journey.
  • True Sustainability: Every journey you take has a positive impact on the local economy and the environment.
